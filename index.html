Accomplishments

Our Product

KHETI, an agri-food tech digital platform, is a flagship product of DV Excellus. It is an integrated web and mobile-based platform with three modules: KHETI Farm, Farm Management Tool, KHETI Food.

It integrates the entire value chain in food production and consumption, primarily delivering services to two major actors in the food systems– FARMERS and CONSUMERS, while other agri-ecosystem players such as cooperatives, development agencies, governments, products/service providers etc. can use the platform to interact and engage with the major actors.
